### Prompt Text

```text
Research paper on AI's impact on K-12 classroom efficiency

Target audience:
Taleemi administrators jo schools mein AI adoption evaluate kar rahe hain

Focus:
Teacher workload kam karna aur student learning outcomes behtar banana

Success criteria:
- Kam az kam 3 ya zyada AI applications ka wazeh taur par hawala
- 8 ya us se zyada peer-review academic sources ka istimaal
- Reader research ke baad classroom AI ka ROI samajh aur explain kar sake
- Har claim evidence aur citation ke saath supported ho

Constraints:
- Word count: 3000–5000 words
- Format: Markdown source ke saath APA style citations
- Sources: Peer-review journals (pichlay 10 saalon ke andar published)
- Timeline: Completion duration 2 weeks maximum

Not building:
- AI field ka mukammal literature review nahi hoga
- Specific AI products/vendors ka muqabla nahi hoga
- Ethical concerns ka discussion is paper ka हिस्सा nahi (yeh alag paper hoga)
```
